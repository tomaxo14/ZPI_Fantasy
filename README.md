# FantasyEkstraliga

This is a Fantasy Speedway game where you can become a manager and set up your own team squad. 
You can buy and sell players on transfer market using a limited budget and following the rules. 
Your team scores points according to how the players perform in the real competition. 
You can compete with your friends and all the players online because there is ranking of all the teams. 
Additionally you can also preview stats of your own team and particular players.
